Persian Cats

If you look below you should see a picture of a ginger cat.

The Persian cat was a favorite of the French nobility.
The Persian cat has a long, thick coat with individual hairs 4-6 inches (10-15 centimeters) in length.
The colors of its coat run the full spectrum from solid, snowy white to wild, multi-tone combinations of tabby, tortoiseshell, patches and more.
